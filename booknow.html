Downstream

Join the adventure

We have several areas where it is possible to carry out this activity, generally in spring and summer season. These drops of rivers are carried out especially in the San Pedro and Fuy rivers, both with grades with difficulty levels from III to IV grades. you can perform this activity through our tour operators.

The commune of Panguipulli receives the name "Sietelagos" due to to the seven lakes that the commune owns: Lake Riñihue, Lake Panguipulli, Lake Pullinque, Lake Pellaifa, Lake Calafquén, Lake Neltume and Lake Pirehueico. We can visit these seven lakes on a tour that includes a tour of the entire territory community Through this route we access viewpoints, thermal centers, parks, reserves, rivers and volcanoes.

More Than Just The Thrill

This exciting tour starts at the Rafting Center and Kayak from Conecta Patagonia, located 45 km from Panguipulli Road to Neltume. This is where all the equipment and instructions necessary for the activity. Once equipped we move to the bridge sector Huilo Huilo where the descent begins, which has a Approximate duration of one hour, ending in the same Camp where it all started. With about 8km of crystalline waters and entertaining rapids, where We also have the option of bathing and jumping into the water from a rock 7 meters high. Our guides professionals make this adventure an activity very entertaining and safe.
